ONE BRAND TWO STORIES

Carhartt, the original workwear brand founded in Detroit, Michigan USA in 1889 has been making enduring products to serve an protect hardworking people ever since. Gear that's born out of respect for the hard-earned dollar to be high quality, extremely durable, and forever counted on to function in the worse conditions and any weather, because Carhartt builds gear that has your back all day, every day, 24/7.

WORK IN PROGRESS

Beginning roughly 100 years after Carhartt began making bibs in the USA, Carhartt WIP started modifying these classic products to serve a European audience. To this day, Carhartt WIP adapts iconic Carhartt gear for those seeking refined design and style that remains ture to the origins of the brand.

The Garment has earned a reputation for its triple seams and heavy cotton fabric. The shade faded over time but remained attractive. It is this ability to wear out and at the same time look fresh that led to the fact that the brand was liked by representative of various subcultures.
